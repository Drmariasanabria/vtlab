Teaching + Research

Classroom experimentation with a research spine

VT Lab is designed for classroom implementation and research into educational technology, vibe-coded resources, gamification, learner experience, and digital pedagogy. Each resource can be adapted for teaching moments, piloting, feedback, and reflective analysis.

Data Collection

Pre/post feedback that travels with each room

Escape rooms can include a pre-task questionnaire and post-task questionnaire, stored locally in the browser and exportable as classroom or research-oriented reports.
